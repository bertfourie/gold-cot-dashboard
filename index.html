Reading the COT Index: Commercials >80 → bullish signal (big hedgers accumulating longs) · <20 → bearish signal. Managed Money >80 → crowded long (contrarian caution) · <20 → crowded short (potential reversal). Non-Reportables (retail) >80 → contrarian bearish · <20 → contrarian bullish. Larry Williams recommends trading WITH commercials against managed money and retail extremes.
